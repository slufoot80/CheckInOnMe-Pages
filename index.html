Someone should always know you're okay.

Check In On Me is the home of SafePulse — a personal safety check-in app. Set a timer, and if you don't check in, SafePulse alerts your trusted contacts with your last known location.

Get SafePulse How it works

Built for the moments no one is watching

Set a check-in before a run, a late drive, a solo hike, or a long day alone. If you don't tap “I'm OK” before the timer runs out, SafePulse reaches the people you chose.

⏳

Automatic Check-Ins

Choose your interval. SafePulse runs quietly in the background until you need it.

🚨

SOS Button

One tap immediately alerts every emergency contact you've added, with your location.

🔒

100% Private

All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no analytics.

🔋

Low-Battery Warning

If your phone gets dangerously low, SafePulse warns your circle so they know you may go offline.

📞

Auto-Call on SOS

SafePulse can automatically dial your first emergency contact when SOS is triggered.

👥

Trusted Circles

Pick the family and friends who get your check-in status and alerts.

How it works

Simple setup, quiet protection. Get going in a couple of minutes.

1

Set your schedule

Choose how often you want to check in — every few hours, once a day, or a custom interval.

2

Add your contacts

Pick the trusted people who'll be notified if you miss a check-in or trigger an alert.

3

Live your life

Go about your day. SafePulse runs in the background, ready the moment you need it.
